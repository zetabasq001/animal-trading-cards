Royal Python

The Royal Python is a popular pet for hobbyist and via selective reproduction new colors and patterns have been designed in captivity by enthusiast. Breeders combine naturally occurring variants found in nature to produce what are called designer morphs.

  • Scientific Name: Python Regius
  • Wild Habitat: West/Central Africa
  • Maximum Length: 6 Feet
  • Conservation Status: Least Concern

The Royal Python is a species of python that is primarily terresterial and a non-venomous constrictor that preys on small mammals. It has a defense strategy when stressed to curl into a ball with its head and neck positioned in its center; hence, the reason why it is also called the Ball Python.
